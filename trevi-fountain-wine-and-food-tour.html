Trevi Fountain Neighboorhood Wine and Food Tour

Delicious and spectacular food tasting experience around the most famous fountain in the world. On this 4 hours food tour you will visit the most interesting attractions of the area while stopping and eating at more than 7 traditional eateries and restaurants. You will also visit a farmers market and learn about fresh ingredients of Roman cuisine. It is a progressive lunch with more food than you would expect.

Tour Description

Sit and sightsee in different locations of Rome after sunset!

Italy is especially famous worldwide for its food. On this 4-hours evening tour we will introduce you to the best dishes from north to south Italy, and typical roman dishes as well. Carefully selected, each stop will provide an unforgettable taste right next to stunning historic sites. While you visit genuine and small family-run business shops to savor authentic food you will also learn what makes them unique, how their ingredients are produced, as well as their history and curiosities.

Discover all the history and facts behind the Jewish Ghetto area while you taste the famous fried artichokes, known as “Carciofi alla giudìa” one of the best-known dishes of Roman Jewish cuisine. We will also visit one of the oldest and best bakeries in town, from 1824, to taste delicious crunchy pizza! After we will take you to a trip through the finest cuts of cured meats, you will savor the best salami and prosciutto from Norcia and indulge the best Italian cheese such as Pecorino Romano and Parmigiano. A rich dinner will be served right after!

Typical roman dishes will be the main courses all paired with excellent wine! As you move from one restaurant to the other, our experienced guide will share facts and histories about traditional Roman cuisine as you stroll through these charming districts. This tour is a well-designed combination of a breath-taking sightseeing attractions with food and wine tasting. Our tour begins with a typical Italian appetizer and ends with authentic Italian coffee, addictive tiramisù and a hand-made gelato!
