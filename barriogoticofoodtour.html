Tour Description

Enjoy a 2.5 h food and art immersion in the heart of Barcelona around the Cathedral, the gothic quarter and El Born.

It is a great combination of sightseeing and tasting starting from the famous Boqueria market and Rambla street continuing to the Kings Palace, Santa Maria del Pi and other must-see monuments in the Born district.

You will taste the original Paella, the Crema Catalana and other typical products of Barcelona and Catalunya Region.

Do not eat before the tour, wear comfortable shoes and do not forget your camera!

What's includes

More than 5 eateries and many monuments and sites on a tasty tour led by a top-rated local guide.
