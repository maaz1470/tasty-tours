Tour Description

Eat, drink and sightsse the Roman culture and cuisine!

Discover this picturesque district and fall in love with the atmosphere of this delightful area! Our local and expert guide will take you to the hidden treasures and will guide you to the highlights of this neighborhood. If you don't have any familiarity with this district, it is easy to get lost or to fall into low quality food: this is why, having an expert guide with you, may help to focus the best places where to spend an unforgettable time!

The tour includes around 10 different food tastings, you will taste one of the best pizza in town, savor greatest salumi and prosciutto like Parma and cheese like Parmiggiano Reggiano. Also as part of the tour you will try local roman pastries from a local family bakery from 1940, all that will be probably accompanied by the music of one of the street performers, that make this area one-of-a-kind.

While you walk from one food stop to another, you will be showed the best highlights of this unique place, this area in known for its history and atmosphere: Piazza Trilussa, that winds along the countless alleys, the Basilica of Santa Maria, and the Tiber Island, one of the tiniest island in the world, whose origins, related to the most ancient legends, are still a mystery. At the end of the tour you will enjoy an artisanal gelato from one of the best shops in town where ingredients are 100% natural. There might be changes accordingly to the season you are taking the tour (many of our dishes are based on fresh season products).
